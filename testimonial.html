Dan George

"Alex was very thoughtful and open when working with me to build my banjo. He asked for my input when making decisions throughout the process and made me feel like I could also ask him questions about details that I wasn’t familiar with.

The result was great! The banjo has a beautiful sound and is wonderful to play! My feelings about the banjo are echoed by other players too, I’ve lent my banjo out to others over the past few years, several of whom have then gone on to order a Fundy Banjo for themselves."

Matt Tingley

“I had a very positive experience with Alex and Fundy Banjos. He resurrected my banjo by fixing the neck and replacing the corroded strings with fresh ones. I love his attention to detail and efficiency, thanks again Alex!”

Pat Madden

"Working with Alex at Fundy Banjos was a real treat. My partner and I both started playing tenor banjo a few years ago, but we were having trouble sourcing a decent tenor banjo in Nova Scotia, so when we discovered that we could have one built we jumped at the opportunity!

Alex was very accommodating with how we wanted our banjo to look and feel. We even got to pick out the hardware and the the inlay design on the head of the banjo. We love the tone and playability of the instrument. The banjo is an open back 17-fret tenor banjo with a very mellow and warm tone. Definitely worth the wait!"
