Welcome to Fundy Banjos, where I craft banjos with a passion for woodworking and instrument making. Over the past five years, I've honed my skills, turning a personal quest for soulful banjos into a dedicated workshop in Centerville, Nova Scotia.

Each banjo is a product of my obsession for continual improvement. I specialize in customization, tailoring instruments to unique aesthetic and sonic specifications. In the quiet morning hours, you'll find me in the workshop, with a cup of coffee and my cat, fueled by a desire to make each banjo play and sound better than the last.

Thank you for joining me on this journey. Whether you're a musician seeking a personalized instrument or an admirer of craftsmanship, explore Fundy Banjos and discover instruments crafted with a soul.

- Alex Nemet
