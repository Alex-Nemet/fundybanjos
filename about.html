About Me

Welcome to Fundy Banjos. I'm Alex Nemet, the craftsman behind each banjo. With a love for woodworking and a passion for instrument making, I've spent the last five years refining my skills and improving my building process. This journey has brought me to a place where I find immense joy and confidence in presenting my banjos to fellow musicians.

What began as a personal quest for a soulful banjo (a quality often elusive in readily available factory-made instruments) has grown into a dedicated banjo workshop in Centerville, nestled in the heart of the Annapolis Valley near the Bay of Fundy, Nova Scotia, Canada.

Crafting banjos for me is an obsession and a journey of continual improvement. I pour my heart into each instrument, fueled by a desire to make them play and sound better with every creation. Customization isn't just a service I offer; it's a deep passion. I specialize in tailoring banjos to unique aesthetic and sonic specifications, setting my workshop apart as a hub for personalized craftsmanship.

In the quiet hours of the morning, you'll find me in the workshop with a cup of coffee and my cat, working diligently to bring a new banjo to life. I take pleasure in forging connections with the musicians for whom I create these instruments, crafting something unique and personally tailored to each individual.

Thank you for joining me on this journey. Whether you're a fellow musician seeking the perfect instrument for your needs or someone who appreciates the art of craftsmanship, I invite you to explore the offerings of Fundy Banjos and discover banjos with a soul.
