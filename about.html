About Me

Welcome to Fundy Banjos. I'm Alex Nemet, the dedicated craftsman behind each banjo. With a love for woodworking and a commitment to the art of instrument making, I've spent the last five years refining my skills and improving my building process. This journey has brought me to a place where I not only find immense joy but also confidence in presenting my banjos to fellow musicians.

What started as a personal need for an banjo with some soul (which I had a hard time finding in any readily available factory made instrument), has evolved into a full-fledged banjo workshop in Centerville, Nova Scotia, Canada. Which lies in the heart of the Annapolis Valley, near the Bay of Fundy.

Crafting banjos for me is not just a job; it's an impassioned journey of continual improvement. I pour my heart into each instrument, fueled by a relentless desire to make them sound better and better with every creation. Customization isn't just a service I offer; it's a profound love. I specialize in tailoring banjos to unique specifications, setting my workshop apart as a dedicated hub for personalized craftsmanship.

In the quiet hours of the morning, you'll find me in the workshop with a coffee, working diligently to bring each banjo to life. This commitment to quality extends to my customer relationships, where I draw on years of experience, mentorship, and a continuous pursuit of innovation.

Thank you for joining me on this journey. Whether you're a fellow musician seeking the perfect instrument for you or someone who appreciates the art of craftsmanship, I invite you to explore the world of Fundy Banjos and discover banjos with a soul.
